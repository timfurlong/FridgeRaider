No need to go to the grocery

Cooking great food shouldn't take up your whole day. Skip the trip to the store and cook only the recipes that you can make right away. That way you can save yourself some money, time, and hassle.

Change the way you shop

With FridgeRaider, you don't need to think about what you're going to make with everything you put in your cart. If you like onions, get onions. If you need some more green vegetables in your diet, buy a few of your favorites. Let us take care of how you're going to prepare it.

Search over a million recipes

FridgeRaider searches the world's largest database of recipes at Yummly.com. Yummly includes recipes from over one hundred different sources, like: AllRecipes, Epicurious, EveryDay with Rachael Ray, Martha Stewart, MyRecipes, Serious Eats, and Taste of Home.
